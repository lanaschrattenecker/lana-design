#DreamYourEUunlimited – EU Video Project

As part of the official #DreamYourEUunlimited 2023 video competition hosted at Salzburg’s schools, our group created a short film envisioning a borderless and creative future for Europe.

About the competition:
The goal was to encourage students to dream freely and depict their vision of Europe in a video. Creativity and thematic interpretation were completely open, with emphasis on future-thinking, unity, and expression.

My Role & Achievements:
As a key member of the film crew, I contributed to our short film, which earned 4th place out of over 500 participants in the official EU #DreamYourEUunlimited competition. The project was highly praised for its aesthetic quality and visual design, highlighting our commitment to creative excellence.

In addition to my creative contributions, I brought strong organizational skills and strategic thinking to the team. My ability to identify unique opportunities and develop impactful marketing ideas helped ensure our project stood out. These skills, combined with my collaborative approach, were essential to achieving a polished and memorable final result.

This experience has reinforced my passion for blending creativity with strategy to deliver compelling visual stories that leave a lasting impression.

🎥 Watch the video on YouTube

Project Requirements

  • Open theme: Dreaming of a limitless European future
  • Free creative format – video up to 2 minutes
  • Team collaboration encouraged
  • Target group: high school students across Salzburg

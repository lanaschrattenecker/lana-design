About me - In depth

In my professional and personal development, I combine depth, strategy and aesthetics – qualities that are not only reflected in my career, but also in the archetypes with which I identify. As INFJ – the advocate – my focus is on visionary thinking, empathy and a clear inner mission. But my path is not a quiet one – it is purposeful, passionate and characterised by inner strength.

With the ambition of a Slytherin (house 1), the thirst for knowledge of a Ravenclaw (house 2) and the diplomatic elegance of a double Libra (both zodiac sign and ascendant), I seek balance between intuition and intellect, between leadership and compassion. I am a Queen (Soldier, Poet, King) – less ruler, more guardian of order and vision.

My affiliation with the Aphrodite Cabin (Percy Jackson Cabin) and the Night Court of the ACOTAR world refers to my ability to combine beauty with depth – be it in interpersonal communication, in design or in strategic decisions.

I am someone who loves what I do – and who does not shy away from combining passion and logic.

In a world of Divergent, I would be Dauntless – brave, determined, ready to stand up for what I believe in. My green lightsaber (Star Wars) symbolises harmony, integrity and healing.

I don't think in categories – I cross them. Whether as a human from Middle-earth (Lord of the Rings) or as a Targaryen (Game of Thrones): I combine down-to-earthness with foresight, intuition with strategy and idealism with a realising spirit.

Although Hercules is my absolute favourite film from the Disney universe – because of its strength, humour and touching message about true greatness – I see myself most closely in Belle: inquisitive, independent, reflective and with a deep love of books and the dialogue between heart and mind.

In the Black Clover universe, I belong to the Black Bulls – unconventional, passionate, fierce, loyal. In Demon Slayer, I would fight alongside the pillars, as Tsuguko of the Pillar of Flame – Kyojuro Rengoku.

And in Attack on Titan? No question – I would be a scout. I would fight Titans with conviction, an eye for the essentials and unshakeable determination.

I love volleyball – because of the dynamism, the energy, the teamwork. And perhaps also because it's like me: quiet, observant, introverted at first glance. But as soon as confidence arises, I burst out – with passion, expressiveness and real fire. I'm not a person who comes out immediately – but anyone who knows me knows that silence is just the beginning for me.

Music accompanies me through every day – not in the background, but as a compass, mirror and creative force. I'm always listening to music, I like to try out new things and often find the words in sounds that you can't say.

I am not an ordinary profile.
I am a story. A mission. An attitude.
I am a flame, a spirit, a heartbeat between million of worlds.

Because I have not just read a thousand stories —
I have lived them, created them, rewritten them, and become part of them.
Between the lines, I create myself anew — not as a reader, but as the pulse of every plot, the voice behind the page.

So let me create a voice in your story too!
